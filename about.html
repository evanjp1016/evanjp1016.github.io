Design is a nice way to explore my interests. I just seem to have a few too many interests.

About two years ago, I graduated from the University of Michigan where I was introduced to, and wound up studying, UX Design.

Initially intending to study Art and Graphic Design, I found that designing how things work and how people interact with them was what I enjoyed most.

Throughout my time at Michigan, I saw the benefit of understanding not only how to design a product, but also how to use data to inform design decisions and how to actually build a product. I jumped at opportunities to learn development and data analysis.

Outside of work, I find my self tangled in probably too many other projects. I make post-trap music, I’m creating artwork for a documentary about a silent film star, I’m writing a TV show about bus drivers and urban development, I’m working on a rebrand for my hometown city government, y estoy aprendiendo español.

I’m also looking to join a design team in 2020, so feel free to reach out at evanjpesch@gmail.com.
