If you ask me, I’d say that I wear many hats, literally and figuratively.

Some may prefer the term, “design generalist,” but I like baseball, so “utility player” it is. Being a utility player, I’ve been lucky enough to stumble upon many opportunities in many different disciplines.

I have experience in design, development, and marketing for projects in urban informatics, cybersecurity, entertainment, solar car racing, film, and urban design.

In my spare time, I foster a separate slew of personal projects, from making music and short films to designing brands and developing web-apps. More recently, I’ve been learning Spanish, working my way back into printmaking, and writing a TV show, so if you know anyone with a printing press or network deal available, let me know.

I’m looking to join a design team in 2020. Feel free to reach out to me about that as well at evanjpesch@gmail.com.
