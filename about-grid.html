I'm Evan Pesch, a product designer currently self-isolating at home in West Michigan

The past 6 years

While my primary interest and education is in product design, my career so far has bounced around within product design, brand design, and web development with organizations like Merit Network, the Michigan Marching Band, and Data Driven Detroit.

I've been doing this for the past 6 years, much of which overlapped with my time studying UX Design at the University of Michigan and making tens of thousands of sandwiches in an industrial cooler (had to pay for that degree somehow).

Why I do it

I create everything with the goal of making people happy, open, and safe

The scale of the collective term people can vary from the entire world to just one person. By happy, I mean bringing joy or improved mental health. By open, I mean accepting of people and cultures other than their own and receptive to non-harmful ideas and actions other than their own. By safe, I mean secure from the threat of physical, emotional, or digital danger, loss, and harm.
