The Project

Overview

After I applied to the position, went through a short portfolio review, and completed a design challenge, I was given the position as the graphic designer for the University of Michigan Solar Car team. For the next year or so after I was hired (until my future graduation from the university) my work has been featured in the team’s digital and physical marketing outlets, business presentations, sponsor and donor outreach efforts, and large team events.

I was recently instructed to title myself “Director of something.” I decided to go with “Director of Graphic and Visual Design.”
